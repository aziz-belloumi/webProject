Providing a specific number can be challenging as individual caloric needs vary greatly based on factors like age, gender, weight, activity level, and metabolism.
However, as a rough estimate for someone looking to bulk and gain weight, a common starting point is often around 500 calories above their estimated maintenance calories.

Harris-Benedict Equation for the number of calories your body needs per day:
For men ♂ :

88.362 + ( 13.397 × weight in kg ) + ( 4.799 × height in cm ) − ( 5.677 × age in years )


For women ♀ :

447.593 + ( 9.247 × weight in kg ) + ( 3.098 × height in cm ) − ( 4.330 × age in years )
