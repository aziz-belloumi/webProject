hand plank


Start in a kneeling position with your hands planted on the ground.

Pick your knees up off the ground. Hold in this position with a flat back.

forward lunges


Step forward with one leg.

Lower your body until your rear knee nearly touches the ground.

Ensure you remain upright, and your front knee stay above the front foot.

Push off the floor with your front foot until you return to the starting position. Switch legs.

push up front


Place your hands firmly on the ground, directly under shoulders.

Flatten your back so your entire body is straight and slowly lower your body

Draw shoulder blades back and down, keeping elbows tucked close to your body

Exhale as you push back to the starting position.

walking calf raises


Walk forward and push your toe into the ground .

Lift you heel off the ground each step and tense your calf muscle .

Repeat on each leg and walk forward slowly .
